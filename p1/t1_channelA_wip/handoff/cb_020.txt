A fund reorganization involves one target fund transferring all of its assets to an acquiring fund in exchange for shares of such acquiring fund and the assumption by the acquiring fund of all liabilities and obligations of the target fund. Once completed, shareholders of the fund being reorganized (i.e., the target fund) will hold shares of the acquiring fund, and the target fund thereafter is typically dissolved, which will be the case for each Target Fund. A fund reorganization is a common mechanism to convert a mutual fund into an ETF, by reorganizing the mutual fund into an acquiring fund structured as an ETF. A fund reorganization involves one target fund transferring all of its assets to an acquiring fund in exchange for shares of such acquiring fund and the assumption by the acquiring fund of all liabilities and obligations of the target fund. Once completed, shareholders of the fund being reorganized (i.e., the target fund) will hold shares of the acquiring fund, and the target fund thereafter is typically dissolved, which will be the case for each Target Fund. A fund reorganization is a common mechanism to convert a ii mutual fund into an ETF, by reorganizing the mutual fund into an acquiring fund structured as an ETF.
